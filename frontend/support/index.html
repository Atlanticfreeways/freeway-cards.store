üìã Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create my first virtual card? +
To create your first virtual card: 1) Go to your dashboard, 2) Click "Create Card", 3) Choose Visa or Mastercard, 4) Select Instant ($2.99) or Monthly ($4.99), 5) Set your spending limits, 6) Click "Create Card". Your card will be ready instantly!
How do I add funds to my wallet? +
You can add funds using Bitcoin (BTC) or Tether (USDT) with no fees, or use credit/debit cards (2.9% fee) or PayPal (3.5% fee). Go to Wallet ‚Üí Add Funds, select your payment method, and follow the instructions.
What's the difference between Instant and Monthly cards? +
Instant cards cost $2.99 one-time and are perfect for single purchases. Monthly cards cost $4.99/month and can be used multiple times, making them ideal for recurring expenses or frequent shopping.
How secure are virtual cards? +
Virtual cards are extremely secure. Each card has unique numbers separate from your main account, spending limits you control, and can be frozen instantly. They protect your real payment information from merchants.
Are there any hidden fees? +
No hidden fees! We charge only: Card creation fees ($2.99 Instant, $4.99/month Monthly), and payment processing fees (2.9% cards, 3.5% PayPal). Crypto funding is completely free.
My card was declined, what should I do? +
Check: 1) Sufficient card balance, 2) Daily/monthly limits not exceeded, 3) Card is not frozen, 4) Merchant is not blocked in settings. If issues persist, contact support.
